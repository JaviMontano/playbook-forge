# MODAL-SPEC v1.0 -- Internal Structure per Modal Category

> **Entrusted Standard** for the internal structure of every modal in playbooks
> generated by `playbook-forge`. Defines exact section order, required CSS
> classes, bilingual requirements, and CTA patterns for each of the 10 modal
> categories.
>
> Derived from golden reference: `playbook_bu2_agile_pm_excellence_v3.html`
> (69 modals across 10 categories).

---

## Universal Modal Shell

Every modal, regardless of category, MUST use this outer shell:

```html
<div class="modal-overlay" id="modal-f{{SUFFIX}}">
  <div class="modal-box">
    <button class="modal-close" onclick="closeModal()" aria-label="Cerrar / Close">&times;</button>
    <div class="modal-header">
      <h3><span class="es">{{TITLE_ES}}</span><span class="en">{{TITLE_EN}}</span></h3>
      <small><span class="es">{{SUBTITLE_ES}}</span><span class="en">{{SUBTITLE_EN}}</span></small>
    </div>
    <div class="modal-body">
      <!-- CATEGORY-SPECIFIC CONTENT -->
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
```

### Universal Rules

- `id` follows `modal-f{suffix}` pattern (see PLAYBOOK-SPEC 3.3)
- `modal-close` button: always first child of `modal-box`, always has `aria-label="Cerrar / Close"`
- `modal-header h3`: ALWAYS bilingual (ES/EN spans)
- `modal-header small`: bilingual subtitle with tool name + duration + frequency
- `modal-body`: category-specific content (defined below)
- All text inside `modal-body` MUST be bilingual (ES/EN span pairs)

## Category 1: Flow Modal (`modal-f{1-13}`)

> **Golden reference count**: 13 modals (`modal-f1` through `modal-f13`)
> **ID pattern**: `modal-f{N}` where N = 1-13
> **openModal call**: `openModal('{N}')`

### Internal Structure (10 sections, ALL required)

```html
<div class="modal-body">

  <!-- 1. PURPOSE CALLOUT -->
  <div class="callout callout-warning">
    <strong class="es">&#10067; Para que lo usas?</strong>
    <strong class="en">&#10067; What is it for?</strong>
    <p class="es">{{PURPOSE_ES}} <!-- min 40 words --></p>
    <p class="en">{{PURPOSE_EN}} <!-- min 40 words --></p>
  </div>

  <!-- 2. HOW IT WORKS HEADING -->
  <h4 class="es">Como funciona</h4>
  <h4 class="en">How it works</h4>

  <!-- 3. TIMELINE (3-5 steps with time markers) -->
  <div class="timeline">
    <div class="timeline-item">
      <div class="timeline-dot">1</div>
      <div class="timeline-content">
        <h3><span class="wk">{{TIME_RANGE}}</span></h3>
        <p class="es">{{STEP_ES}}</p>
        <p class="en">{{STEP_EN}}</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <!-- Repeat for 3-5 steps -->
  </div>

  <!-- 4. OUTPUT HEADING -->
  <h4 class="es">Que obtienes al final</h4>
  <h4 class="en">What you get at the end</h4>

  <!-- 5. OUTPUT DESCRIPTION -->
  <p class="es">{{OUTPUT_DESC_ES}} <!-- min 25 words --></p>
  <p class="en">{{OUTPUT_DESC_EN}} <!-- min 25 words --></p>

  <!-- 6. DEFINITION OF DONE CALLOUT -->
  <div class="callout" style="border-color:var(--sofka-orange);background:var(--sofka-orange-dim);">
    <strong class="es">Como sabes que lo hiciste bien</strong>
    <strong class="en">How you know you did it right</strong>
    <p class="es">{{DOD_ES}} <!-- min 20 words --></p>
    <p class="en">{{DOD_EN}} <!-- min 20 words --></p>
  </div>

  <!-- 7. PROGRESSION HEADING -->
  <h4 class="es">Cuando vas a notar la diferencia</h4>
  <h4 class="en">When you'll notice the difference</h4>

  <!-- 8. WEEK PROGRESSION -->
  <p class="es"><strong>Semana 1-2:</strong> {{W1_ES}} <strong>Semana 4:</strong> {{W4_ES}} <strong>Semana 8:</strong> {{W8_ES}}</p>
  <p class="en"><strong>Week 1-2:</strong> {{W1_EN}} <strong>Week 4:</strong> {{W4_EN}} <strong>Week 8:</strong> {{W8_EN}}</p>

  <!-- 9. (OPTIONAL) EDGE CASE CALLOUTS -->
  <!-- Additional callouts for special considerations -->

  <!-- 10. CTA LINKS -->
  <div class="modal-cta">
    <a class="gem-link pulse" href="{{PRIMARY_GEM_URL}}" target="_blank">{{PRIMARY_TOOL}}</a>
    <a class="gem-link" href="{{SECONDARY_GEM_URL}}" target="_blank">{{SECONDARY_TOOL}}</a>
  </div>

</div>
```

### Validation Rules

| Check | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Timeline steps | 3-5 `timeline-item` elements |
| Time markers | Each step has `span.wk` with time range (e.g. "0-5 min") |
| Callouts | Minimum 2 (purpose + DoD) |
| CTA | At least 1 `gem-link`, primary with `.pulse` class |
| Bilingual | Every `p`, `strong`, `h4` has ES/EN pairs |
| Word count | Purpose paragraph min 40 words per language |

## Category 2: Anti-pattern Modal (`modal-fap{1-13}`)

> **Golden reference count**: 13 modals (`modal-fap1` through `modal-fap13`)
> **ID pattern**: `modal-fap{N}` where N = 1-13
> **openModal call**: `openModal('ap{N}')`

### Internal Structure (5 sections, ALL required)

```html
<div class="modal-body">

  <!-- 1. WHY IT HAPPENS -->
  <div class="callout callout-info">
    <strong class="es">&#10067; Por que ocurre</strong>
    <strong class="en">&#10067; Why it happens</strong>
    <p class="es">{{WHY_ES}} <!-- min 30 words --></p>
    <p class="en">{{WHY_EN}} <!-- min 30 words --></p>
  </div>

  <!-- 2. HOW TO FIX HEADING -->
  <h4 class="es">Como corregirlo</h4>
  <h4 class="en">How to fix it</h4>

  <!-- 3. REMEDIATION TIMELINE (3 steps) -->
  <div class="timeline">
    <div class="timeline-item">
      <div class="timeline-dot">1</div>
      <div class="timeline-content">
        <h3><span class="wk es">{{STEP_TITLE_ES}}</span><span class="wk en">{{STEP_TITLE_EN}}</span></h3>
        <p class="es">{{STEP_ES}}</p>
        <p class="en">{{STEP_EN}}</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <!-- 3 steps total -->
  </div>

  <!-- 4. EARLY WARNING CALLOUT -->
  <div class="callout" style="border-color:var(--sofka-orange);background:var(--sofka-orange-dim);">
    <strong class="es">Senal de alerta temprana</strong>
    <strong class="en">Early warning signal</strong>
    <p class="es">{{SIGNAL_ES}}</p>
    <p class="en">{{SIGNAL_EN}}</p>
  </div>

  <!-- 5. (NO CTA -- anti-pattern modals do not have gem links) -->

</div>
```

### Validation Rules

| Check | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Timeline steps | Exactly 3 `timeline-item` elements |
| Callouts | Exactly 2 (why + early warning) |
| CTA | NONE -- anti-pattern modals never have gem links |
| Bilingual | Every `p`, `strong`, `h4` has ES/EN pairs |
| Tone | Corrective, not judgmental -- focus on behavior change |
