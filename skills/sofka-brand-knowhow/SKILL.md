| Body (DS v5.1 + Manual) | Inter | system-ui, sans-serif | [DOC-MANUAL-2024] |

### Typography Rules (Manual de Marca 2024)

- Minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1 (text/background) [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
- Large-scale text (18pt or 14pt bold): minimum 3:1 [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
- Maximum 3 font sizes or weights per layout [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
- Sentence case for headlines [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
- Keep copy flush left [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
- Minimum digital: 20px height [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
- Minimum print: 0.22" (5.5mm) [DOC-MANUAL-2024]

### Clash Grotesk

"Featuring many fine details, moderate contrast, and slightly unusual anatomy, the typeface can be a loud and proud hero for all sorts of designs. As a long-standing core brand element, it brings Sofka's identity to our communications." [DOC-MANUAL-2024]

## 11. Illustration Style Guide

"Our illustration style may look simple, but it shows a careful understanding of our philosophy. Using simple geometric shapes, a strong grid, and mathematically derived proportions, we can create a wide variety of engaging illustrations. Our Kaizen philosophy inspires abstract illustration and visual metaphors." [DOC-MANUAL-2024]

### Rules

- Use simple geometric shapes [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
- Apply strong mathematical grid [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
- Use mathematically derived proportions [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
- Create abstract illustrations and visual metaphors [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
- Draw inspiration from Kaizen continuous improvement philosophy [DOC-MANUAL-2024]

### Grid System

"Without organization, a message might get lost. Grids might be invisible, but they're essential — for balance, emphasis, and understanding. Our grid system provides a platform for holistic design across all applications." [DOC-MANUAL-2024]
